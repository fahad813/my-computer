About Our Company

Learn about our journey, values, and the team behind My Computer

Our Story

Founded in 2016, My Computer began as a small computer repair shop with a vision to provide reliable technology solutions to our local community. Over the years, we've grown into a full-service I.T. and networking company serving businesses and residential clients across the region.

Our Expertise

With over a decade of experience in the industry, our team of certified professionals brings deep technical knowledge and practical problem-solving skills to every project. We specialize in:

  • Network design and implementation
  • Security camera installation and monitoring
  • Business I.T. infrastructure
  • System maintenance and support

Our Values

At My Computer, we believe in:

  • Integrity: Honest advice and transparent pricing
  • Quality: Using only the best equipment and practices
  • Reliability: Being there when you need us
  • Innovation: Staying ahead with the latest technologies

Meet Our Team

Our team consists of certified network engineers, security specialists, and I.T. professionals who are passionate about technology and committed to customer satisfaction. We invest in continuous training to ensure we can handle any technical challenge you may face.
